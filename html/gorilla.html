Gorilla Trekking Safaris in Uganda

Gorilla and chimpanzee trekking are vigorous activities that involve hiking through forests and jungles to observe primates in their natural habitat. This is a breath-taking experience, not to be missed. Uganda is home to over half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas, making it a popular destination for gorilla trekking. Chimpanzee trekking is also unique, as Uganda has one of the highest concentrations of chimpanzees. These activities offer a close encounter with the primates and support conservation efforts around the communities.

5 Days Wildlife & Gorillas Safari Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park

Arrive at Entebbe international airport, meet your driver/guide and transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park in the foothills of the mystical Rwenzori mountain ranges. Queen Elizabeth National Park is Uganda's second-largest National park.

Accommodation: Mweya Safari Lodge / Enganzi Lodge / Bush Lodge

Day 2: Game Drive and Boat Cruise

After a cup of tea/coffee, go for a dawn game drive to catch up with the early risers and predator feeding before returning to their nests. Expect encounters with Lions, hyenas, herds of elephants, buffaloes, jackals, bush pigs, bushbucks, and many more.

In the afternoon, join a boat cruise at the Kazinga channel to see animals like Hippos, huge Nile crocodiles, Elephants, and Buffaloes basking on the shores. Birdlife is prolific with over 600 species.

Day 3: Ishasha Tree Climbing Lions and Transfer to Bwindi

Morning game drive in the Ishasha sector, famous for tree-climbing lions, before transferring to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park.

Day 4: Gorilla Trekking

Early morning briefing at park headquarters before starting your gorilla trek. Spend one hour with the gorillas once found.

Day 5: Return to Kampala/Entebbe

Breakfast and drive back to Kampala or Entebbe for your departure flight.

7 Days Rwanda & Uganda Adventure Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kampala - City Tour

Meet your tour guide and transfer to Kampala. Enjoy a city tour visiting Kasubi Tombs, Wamala tombs, Bahai temple, Namugongo Martyrs Shrine, Uganda Museum, and craft shops.

Day 2: Kampala to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

After breakfast, depart for Bwindi Forest. Stop at the Uganda equator for photographs and lunch in Mbarara. Enjoy scenic views of Ankole cattle farms, banana plantations, and Kigezi hills.

Accommodation: Gorilla Forest Camp, Gorilla Mist Camp, Four Gorillas Lodge, or Mahogany Springs Lodge

Day 3: Gorilla Tracking in Bwindi

Early morning briefing before trekking to meet one of the 17 gorilla families in Bwindi Forest. Spend an hour with gorillas. Optional evening Batwa community visit.

Day 4: Bwindi to Parc National des Volcans

Drive to Kisoro and cross the border to Ruhengeri in Rwanda, enjoying scenic views en route.

Accommodation: Mountain Gorilla View Lodge, Kwitonda Lodge, Virunga Lodge, Bisate Lodge, or Le Bambou Gorilla Lodge

Day 5: Gorilla Tracking in Volcanoes Park & Transfer to Lake Bunyonyi

Second gorilla trek in Volcanoes National Park. After spending an hour with the gorillas, transfer to Lake Bunyonyi.

Accommodation: Arcadia Cottages or Bunyonyi Overland Camp

Day 6: Lake Bunyonyi Activities

Relaxing day at Lake Bunyonyi. Activities include boat cruises to various islands including the historic punishment island, birding, cycling, swimming, and canoeing.

Day 7: Lake Bunyonyi to Kampala

Drive back to Kampala with lunch en-route. Transfer to the airport or hotel.

3 Days Uganda Gorilla Trekking Itinerary

Day One: Kampala to Bwindi National Park

Pick up from your hotel in Kampala or Entebbe Airport by our professional English-speaking tour guide and hit the road to western Uganda. En-route stopover at the Equator for photographs, lunch in Mbarara town (Igongo Cultural Center) before continuing through the Kigezi region, famously known as the "Switzerland of Africa", characterized by Majestic rolling hills, deep valleys, and winding roads.

Accommodation: Rushaga Gorilla Lodge, Lake Mutanda Resort, or Chameleon Hill Lodge

Day Two: Gorilla Tracking Experience

Start early with a heavy breakfast at the lodge, and assemble at the park headquarters for a briefing about gorilla trekking in Bwindi National Park. You must be physically fit to go gorilla trekking in the Bwindi forest. The trek takes 2 to 7 hours depending on the movement of these Gentle Giants in their natural habitat! Highly recommended to hire a porter during the hike and carry your backpacker with your packed lunch and camera gear. This is one way gorilla tourism creates employment opportunities for the local communities.

The thrill of encountering these gentle Giants in their natural habitat offers a lifetime adventure experience and erases all the poor memories you went through while searching for them. Spend an hour with the gorillas, learn about their lifestyles, take pictures, and enjoy this lifetime experience.

Retire for lunch, refresh at the lodge, and drive to the beautiful Lake Bunyonyi for dinner and overnight at Bunyonyi Overland Resort, Birds Nest Resort, or Arcadia Cottages.

Day Three: Reserved for Departure

Breakfast is followed by a boat ride to explore the beautiful Islands on Lake Bunyonyi. Return at around 10 am and, either drive back to Kampala / Entebbe Airport for your departure flight (8-hour drive), or connect to Kigali International Airport in Rwanda through the Katuna border (3-hr drive). Here we have an opportunity to visit the Gisozi genocide memorial museum in Kigali.

Included in the price:
  • A gorilla tracking permit at US$800 per person
  • Private transport in a comfortable 4×4 safari vehicle
  • Services of a professional full-time English-speaking driver/tour guide
  • 2 Night accommodation and meals (Full board)
  • Enroute meals and bottled mineral water for refreshment
  • All tour activities as per the itinerary
Excluded:
  • International flights
  • Visas
  • Other personal expenses like tips for staff and beverages

3 Days Gorilla Habituation Experience Itinerary

Day One: Arrival and Transfer to Bwindi

Meet and greet at your preferred starting point (Kigali or Kampala). Drive to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park through scenic landscapes. Check-in at your lodge for dinner and overnight stay.

Accommodation: Nkuringo Bwindi Gorilla Lodge, Rushaga Gorilla Camp, or Gorilla Safari Lodge

Day Two: Gorilla Habituation Experience

Wake up for heavy breakfast, drive to the park headquarters with packed lunch and water. After a 30-minute briefing about gorilla habituation experience, start your hike at 8:00 am to meet a wild gorilla family. You will spend four hours with the gorillas, training them to get used to human presence. The whole experience may take up a full day depending on the length of the gorilla trek.

Return to your lodge for dinner and overnight stay.

Day Three: Drive back to Kigali/Kampala – Departure

A relaxed morning, followed by breakfast and transfer back to Kigali or Kampala where your tour ends. Alternatively, we can do gorilla tracking in the morning and then drive back to Kigali where the tour ends.
